Personality and Temperament


California Spangled cats are affectionate, social, and playful. They are known for being friendly and love interacting with their human companions and other pets. They are playful and enjoy engaging activities, making them a great choice for families with children. Despite their wild appearance, they are gentle, sociable, and docile companions.



Nutrition Grooming Exercise Health

Nutrition

California Spangled cats don't require a special diet, but providing them with a high-quality diet with real meat or fish as the first ingredient is essential. A balanced diet will support their health and luxurious coat.

Grooming

Their short coat requires minimal grooming. Brushing once a week will help prevent matting and reduce shedding. Regular nail trimming and dental care are also important.

Exercise

These cats are active and enjoy interactive play. Providing them with toys, scratching posts, and climbing opportunities is a great way to keep them physically and mentally stimulated.

Health

California Spangled cats are generally healthy but can be prone to certain genetic conditions. Regular vet check-ups and a healthy diet will help keep them in top condition.


History


The California Spangled cat breed was intentionally bred to resemble a leopard. The breed was created by crossing several breeds, including Abyssinian, Siamese, American Shorthair, British Shorthair, Angora cats, Manx, and moggies from Malaya and Egypt. The breed was perfected in 1985 and introduced to the public in 1986 when they graced the cover of the Neiman Marcus Christmas catalog.

California Spangled Cat: Breed Information






COAT


California Spangled cats have soft, plush coats with fine hair.


COLOR


California Spangled cats may be bronze, gold, brown, black, charcoal, silver, red, blue, or white. All colors display well-defined spots, stripes, and rosettes that lend to this cat‚Äôs incredibly wild appearance. High contrast between markings and background color is desirable.



BODY


The California Spangled cat is medium-large, with a muscular body.


HEAD


The head is of medium size and well proportioned to the body. Whisker pads are prominent.


EYES


The eyes are almond-shaped, with at least one eye's width between them. They complement the coat color. "Snow Leopard" white California Spangled cats have blue eyes, while other colors typically display green eyes.


EARS


The ears are medium to small, with rounded tips.


LEGS & PAWS


The legs are powerful and muscular, while the paws are rounded and proportionate to the legs.


TAIL


The tail is long and graceful, with a tapered profile.


Did You Know?

If you are lucky enough to share your home with a California Spangled cat, you'll need to provide them with high places to perch. These cats are excellent climbers and they prefer to watch the action from a high vantage point.
