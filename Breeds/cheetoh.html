Personality and Temperament


The Cheetoh cat is known for its affectionate, energetic, and playful nature. They are intelligent and enjoy interactive play. Cheetoh cats are highly sociable and prefer to be around their human companions, making them great pets for families.



Nutrition Grooming Exercise Health

Nutrition

The Cheetoh cat does well on a balanced diet that includes high-quality protein sources. Fresh food with minimal fillers is always a good choice for this breed. Ensure they have access to clean water at all times.

Grooming

The Cheetoh has a short coat that requires minimal grooming. Brushing once a week will help maintain their coat and minimize shedding. Regular nail trimming, ear cleaning, and dental care are also part of the grooming routine.

Exercise

Cheetoh cats are highly active and enjoy interactive play. Providing them with toys, scratching posts, and climbing opportunities will help keep them physically and mentally stimulated.

Health

Cheetoh cats are generally healthy but can be prone to some genetic conditions. Regular vet checkups will ensure they stay healthy.


History


The Cheetoh cat is a newer hybrid breed created in the United States in 2001 by Carol Dryman. The breed was developed by crossing Bengal cats with Ocicats to create a new cat breed with a gentle, friendly nature and a physical appearance similar to a wildcat. Despite being a relatively new breed, the Cheetoh cat has gained popularity for its unique appearance and affectionate personality.





Did You Know?

The Cheetoh cat is one of the largest and rarest cat breeds, weighing more than 20 pounds. They are known for their high energy levels and playful nature.





Cheetoh Cat: Breed Information






COAT


The Cheetoh cat has a short, sleek coat that is easy to maintain. The coat comes in a variety of spotted and marbled patterns, giving them a wild appearance.


COLOR


Cheetoh cats come in a range of colors, including brown, gold, and silver, with distinctive spots or marble patterns.



BODY


The Cheetoh cat has a large, muscular body with a sleek, athletic build. They are agile and graceful, with a strong and sturdy frame.


HEAD


The head of the Cheetoh cat is broad with a soft, rounded muzzle. They have a well-defined jaw and a gentle expression.


EYES


The eyes of the Cheetoh cat are large and expressive, coming in shades of green or gold that complement their coat.


EARS


The ears of the Cheetoh cat are medium-sized, with a broad base and rounded tips.


LEGS & PAWS


The legs of the Cheetoh cat are strong and muscular, with medium-length limbs and oval-shaped paws.


TAIL


The tail of the Cheetoh cat is medium-length, tapering to a rounded tip. It is proportionate to the body and helps with balance and agility.
