Personality and Temperament


Burmillas are affectionate, playful, and friendly cats that enjoy being around their human family. They are social cats, often seeking attention and enjoying time spent with their owners. Their gentle and sweet-natured personalities make them ideal pets for families and individuals alike. While they are playful and curious, they are not overly demanding and are quite content to relax with their owners.



Nutrition Grooming Exercise Health

Nutrition

Burmillas thrive on a high-protein diet, and like all cats, they should have meat as the first ingredient in their food. A healthy balance of nutrients will help support their coat and overall health.

Grooming

The Burmilla‚Äôs short coat is relatively easy to maintain. Brushing once a week will help reduce shedding and keep the coat shiny. Regular dental care, ear cleaning, and nail trimming are also recommended.

Exercise

Burmillas are playful and active, though they are not as high-energy as some other breeds. They enjoy interactive play and like to chase toys, but they can also be content lounging next to their family. Ensuring they have opportunities to climb and explore is important for their well-being.

Health

The Burmilla is generally a healthy breed. However, like many breeds, they can be prone to certain conditions like hypertrophic cardiomyopathy (HCM) and renal issues. Regular vet checkups and a healthy diet are important for their longevity.


History


The Burmilla is a relatively modern breed, first developed in the UK in the 1980s by crossing a Burmese and a Chinchilla Persian. The breed quickly gained popularity for its striking looks and affectionate personality.

Burmilla Cat: Breed Information






COAT


The Burmilla cat has a short, silky coat with a soft texture.


COLOR


The Burmilla cat has a silver coat, with a shimmering sheen, usually in a shaded pattern.



BODY


The Burmilla cat is medium-sized with a muscular, compact build.


HEAD


The head of the Burmilla cat is rounded with a short, blunt nose.


EYES


The Burmilla cat has large, round eyes, usually green or gold.


EARS


The ears of the Burmilla cat are medium-sized and rounded.


LEGS & PAWS


The legs of the Burmilla cat are medium-length with rounded paws.


TAIL


The Burmilla cat has a medium-length tail that tapers slightly toward the end.
