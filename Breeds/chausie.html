Personality and Temperament


Chausie cats are affectionate, social, and playful. They are known for being friendly and love interacting with their human companions and other pets. They are playful and enjoy engaging activities, making them a great choice for families with children. These cats are also quite intelligent and enjoy mental stimulation.

Chausie cats are generally healthy but can be prone to certain genetic conditions. Regular vet check-ups and a healthy diet will help keep them in top condition.


History


The Chausie is a hybrid breed that originated by breeding individuals from the non-domestic species jungle cat (Felis chaus) with domestic cats (Felis catus). The breed developed naturally among ancient Egyptians and along the rivers of Southeast Asia, with a modern breeding project beginning in the US in the 1990s. The Chausie was first recognized as a domestic breed by The International Cat Association (TICA) in 1995.
