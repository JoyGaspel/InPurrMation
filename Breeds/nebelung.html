Personality and Temperament


Nebelung cats are affectionate, intelligent, and tend to be reserved. They are known for being loyal to their owners but can be shy around strangers. This breed has a calm and gentle nature, often forming strong bonds with their family. They appreciate a calm and structured environment.

Grooming

Their semi-long coat requires regular grooming. Brushing several times a week will help prevent mats and reduce shedding. Regular nail trimming and dental care are also important.

Exercise

These cats are moderately active and enjoy interactive play. Providing them with toys, scratching posts, and climbing opportunities is a great way to keep them physically and mentally stimulated.

Health

Nebelungs are generally healthy cats but can be prone to certain genetic conditions. Regular vet check-ups and a healthy diet will help keep them in top condition.


History


The Nebelung breed originated in the United States in the 1980s, developed by Cora Cobb. The name "Nebelung" comes from the German word "Nebel," meaning "fog" or "mist," which perfectly describes the breed's silky blue semi-long coat with a silvery hue.




Did You Know?

The Nebelung cat's name comes from the German word "Nebel," meaning "fog" or "mist," and the Russian word "nebo," meaning "sky" or "heaven." They are known for their striking green eyes and plush, silvery-blue coats.





Nebelung Cat: Breed Information






COAT


The Nebelung has a semi-long, dense, double-layered coat that is fine and silky in texture. The coat has a silvery sheen that gives the breed its unique appearance.


COLOR


The Nebelung cat coat is a solid blue color with a silvery sheen. The coat color is uniform throughout the body, with no markings or patterns.



BODY


Nebelungs possess a medium-sized, sleek, and muscular build. Overall, there is a look of athleticism and elegance. The average weight for the male Nebelung is 7 to 15 pounds; that for the female is slightly less.


HEAD


The head of the Nebelung cat is a wedge-shaped with a broad, well-defined muzzle. The overall expression is gentle and intelligent.


EYES


Nebelungs have striking green, almond-shaped eyes, accompanied by dark lines that run from the eyes and eyebrows. The eyes are large, expressive, and add to the breed's exotic look.


EARS


Nebelung cats have large, wide-set, triangular ears that are cupped at the base.


LEGS & PAWS


The legs of the Nebelung cat are long, slim, and muscular. The paws are relatively small and oval-shaped.


TAIL


Nebelung cats have a medium-length tail that is thickest at the base and tapers to a point at the end.



Did You Know?

Nebelung cats are known for their striking green eyes and plush, silvery-blue coats. They are believed to bring good luck and prosperity in folklore.
