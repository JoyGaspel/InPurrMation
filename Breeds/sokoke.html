Personality and Temperament


Sokoke cats are affectionate, social, and playful. They are known for being friendly and love interacting with their human companions and other pets. They are playful and enjoy engaging activities, making them a great choice for families with children. These cats are also quite vocal and enjoy conversing with their human companions.

Grooming

Their short coat requires minimal grooming. Brushing once a week will help prevent matting and reduce shedding. Regular nail trimming and dental care are also important.

Exercise

These cats are active and enjoy interactive play. Providing them with toys, scratching posts, and climbing opportunities is a great way to keep them physically and mentally stimulated.

Health

Sokoke cats are generally healthy but can be prone to certain genetic conditions. Regular vet check-ups and a healthy diet will help keep them in top condition.


History


The Sokoke cat breed, also known as the Sokoke Forest cat or the African Shorthair, originated in the coastal region of Kenya, East Africa, specifically from the greater Arabuko-Sokoke Forest Preserve area. It is a naturally occurring native breed that developed mostly without human intervention, though it is now completely domesticated. The Sokoke is considered the rarest breed of domestic cat in the world.




Did You Know?

Sokoke cats are known for their lively personality and intelligence. They are playful and affectionate, making them excellent companions.




Sokoke Cat: Breed Information






COAT


The Sokoke cat has a short, glossy coat that is close-lying and lacks an undercoat. The coat has a unique "wood grain" pattern, which is its most distinctive feature.


COLOR


Sokoke cats typically have a tabby pattern with various shades of brown. The coat pattern is usually marbled or classic tabby, giving it a natural, wild look.



BODY


The Sokoke cat has a slender, athletic body with long legs and a lean build. They are known for their agility and grace.


HEAD


The head of the Sokoke cat is small and slightly rounded with high cheekbones and a well-defined chin.


EYES


The eyes of the Sokoke cat are almond-shaped and can range in color from green to amber, giving them an expressive look.


EARS


The ears of the Sokoke cat are medium to large in size, with a broad base and slightly rounded tips. They are set high on the head and are slightly flared.


LEGS & PAWS


The legs of the Sokoke cat are long and slender, with the hind legs being slightly longer than the front legs. The paws are small and oval-shaped.


TAIL


The tail of the Sokoke cat is long and tapering, with a slight curve at the end.
