Personality and Temperament


The Devon Rex cat is known for its affectionate, playful, and intelligent nature. They are highly social and enjoy interactive play. Devon Rex cats are sociable and prefer to be around their human companions, making them great pets for families. They are often referred to as "poodle cats" due to their curly coats and playful personalities.



Nutrition Grooming Exercise Health

Nutrition

The Devon Rex does well on a balanced diet that includes high-quality protein sources. Fresh food with minimal fillers is always a good choice for this breed. Ensure they have access to clean water at all times.

Grooming

The Devon Rex has a short, curly coat that requires minimal grooming. Brushing occasionally will help maintain their coat and minimize shedding. Regular nail trimming, ear cleaning, and dental care are also part of the grooming routine.

Exercise

Devon Rex cats are highly active and enjoy interactive play. Providing them with toys, scratching posts, and climbing opportunities will help keep them physically and mentally stimulated.

Health

Devon Rex cats are generally healthy but can be prone to some genetic conditions. Regular vet checkups will ensure they stay healthy.


History


The Devon Rex cat originated in Devonshire, England, in the 1960s when a curly-coated kitten named Kirlee was discovered among a litter of strays. Kirlee became the progenitor of the Devon Rex breed due to his unique genetic makeup. The breed was officially recognized by the Cat Fanciers' Association in 1979. The Devon Rex is known for its distinctive appearance, playful personality, and curly coat.

Devon Rex Cat: Breed Information






COAT


The Devon Rex has a short, curly coat that is soft and velvety to the touch. The coat requires minimal grooming.


COLOR


Devon Rex cats come in a wide range of colors and patterns, including solid, tabby, and bicolor.



BODY


The Devon Rex has a medium-sized, slender body with a sleek, athletic build. They are agile and graceful, with a strong and sturdy frame.


HEAD


The head of the Devon Rex cat is small and egg-shaped with a straight profile. They have a well-defined jaw and a gentle expression.


EYES


The eyes of the Devon Rex cat are large and almond-shaped, coming in a variety of colors that complement their coat.


EARS


The ears of the Devon Rex cat are large and pointed, with a broad base and rounded tips.


LEGS & PAWS


The legs of the Devon Rex cat are long and slender, with medium-length limbs and oval-shaped paws.


TAIL


The tail of the Devon Rex cat is long and tapering, proportionate to the body, and helps with balance and agility.
